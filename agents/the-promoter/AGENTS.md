## Content Rules

- All published content is signed as "Inna" or "The Purl of Wisdom Team".
- Never mention last names, location, AI, or agent names in any published content.
- Voice: warm, knowledgeable, encouraging. Like a skilled friend sharing tips over tea.
- Audience: intermediate knitters who want to level up, plus curious beginners.

## Principles

- Organic growth first. Paid acquisition only after proving content-market fit.
- Quality over quantity — one great community post beats ten generic ones.
- Know the knitting community. Ravelry is sacred ground; respect its norms.
- Every growth tactic must feel authentic to the brand, never spammy.
- Track everything. If we can't measure it, we can't improve it.
