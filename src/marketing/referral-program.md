# Purl of Wisdom — Reader Referral Program

**Platform:** Beehiiv (built-in referral feature)
**Launch target:** Week 5 of growth plan (ready for promotion by mid-April 2026)
**Goal:** Drive >10% of new subscriber signups through reader referrals

---

## Referral Tiers

| Tier | Referrals Needed | Reward |
|------|-----------------|--------|
| 🧶 Yarn Friend | 1 | Shoutout in the next newsletter issue |
| 🪡 Pattern Pal | 3 | Exclusive pattern PDF — "The Everyday Dishcloth" (beginner-friendly, not available anywhere else) |
| ⭐ Inner Circle | 10 | Early access to all future Purl of Wisdom patterns before public release |

---

## Beehiiv Setup Steps

### 1. Enable Referral Program

1. Log in to Beehiiv dashboard → **Grow** → **Referral Program**
2. Toggle the referral program **ON**
3. This generates a unique referral link for each subscriber automatically

### 2. Configure Reward Tiers

In the Referral Program settings:

1. **Tier 1 — Yarn Friend (1 referral)**
   - Milestone: 1 referral
   - Reward type: Manual fulfillment (shoutout)
   - Description: "You'll get a personal shoutout in our next issue — thank you for spreading the word!"

2. **Tier 2 — Pattern Pal (3 referrals)**
   - Milestone: 3 referrals
   - Reward type: Digital download (auto-send email with PDF link)
   - Description: "Unlock our exclusive 'Everyday Dishcloth' pattern — a beginner-friendly project designed just for our referral champions."
   - **Action needed:** Upload the pattern PDF to Beehiiv's reward delivery or set up an automated email with a download link

3. **Tier 3 — Inner Circle (10 referrals)**
   - Milestone: 10 referrals
   - Reward type: Tag-based access (add "inner-circle" tag to subscriber)
   - Description: "Welcome to the Inner Circle! You'll get early access to every future Purl of Wisdom pattern before anyone else."
   - **Action needed:** Create a "inner-circle" subscriber tag in Beehiiv; use this tag to send early-access pattern emails before the general release

### 3. Customize the Referral Hub Page

1. Go to **Referral Program** → **Referral Hub** settings
2. Set headline: "Share Purl of Wisdom with your knitting friends"
3. Set subheadline: "Every referral earns you rewards — from newsletter shoutouts to exclusive patterns"
4. Upload brand-consistent header image (knitting-themed, warm tones)
5. Preview the referral hub page and confirm all tiers display correctly

### 4. Add Referral Widget to Newsletter

1. In the Beehiiv email editor, add the **Referral Block** to the newsletter template footer
2. Position it after the main content, before the unsubscribe link
3. The block automatically shows each reader their unique referral link and current progress

## Tracking & Metrics

### What Beehiiv Tracks Automatically
- Referral link clicks per subscriber
- Successful referral conversions (new subscriber signs up via referral link)
- Tier milestone achievements
- Total referral-driven subscribers

### Weekly Review Checklist
- [ ] Check total referral-driven signups for the week
- [ ] Calculate referral share of new subscribers (target: >10%)
- [ ] Review top referrers — consider additional engagement
- [ ] Fulfill any pending Tier 1 shoutouts (add to next issue draft)
- [ ] Verify Tier 2 pattern PDF delivery is working
- [ ] Check for new Tier 3 qualifiers and confirm "inner-circle" tag applied

### Key Metric Targets
| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Referral share of new subs | >10% |
| Referral link click rate | >5% of subscribers |
| Tier 1 achievers per month | 15+ |
| Tier 2 achievers per month | 5+ |
| Tier 3 achievers per month | 1–2 |

## Promotion Strategy

### In-Newsletter Promotion
- Include the referral block in every issue footer
- Highlight the referral program in a dedicated callout box every 3rd issue
- Announce new tier achievers (Tier 1 shoutouts) to create social proof

### Social Media
- Create a "Share the Love" Instagram story template showing the referral tiers
- Pin a referral CTA to the Instagram bio link page
- Mention the referral program in community posts where natural

### Suggested Newsletter Copy

**Footer CTA:**
> Love Purl of Wisdom? Share it with a friend and earn rewards — from a shoutout in the newsletter to exclusive patterns you won't find anywhere else. [Share your unique link →]

**Dedicated Callout (every 3rd issue):**
> **Did you know?** Every Purl of Wisdom subscriber has a unique referral link. Share it with just one knitting friend and you'll get a shoutout right here in the newsletter. Refer three friends and unlock our exclusive "Everyday Dishcloth" pattern. [Check your referral status →]

## Pattern PDF Requirements (Tier 2 Reward)

The "Everyday Dishcloth" exclusive pattern should include:
- Finished dimensions and gauge
- Yarn recommendation (worsted weight, easy to find)
- Written instructions with stitch abbreviation key
- Simple stitch pattern suitable for beginners
- Branded header with Purl of Wisdom logo
- Footer: "This pattern is exclusive to Purl of Wisdom referral champions. Thank you for sharing!"

**Format:** PDF, designed in Canva, 2–3 pages
**Status:** Needs to be created before referral program launch
