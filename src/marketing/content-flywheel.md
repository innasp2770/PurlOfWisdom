## Design Notes

- **Carousels:** Use Canva. Brand colours (warm neutrals + accent). Consistent font pairing. Each slide should be readable standalone.
- **Pinterest pins:** Vertical format (1000x1500px). Bold text overlay. Link always goes to signup page with UTM: `?utm_source=pinterest&utm_medium=pin&utm_campaign=[issue-number]`
- **Instagram posts:** UTM in bio link: `?utm_source=instagram&utm_medium=carousel&utm_campaign=[issue-number]`
- **Tone:** Warm, knowledgeable, approachable. Every post should feel like a friend sharing a tip, not a brand shouting.
- **CTA on every post:** "More in this week's newsletter — link in bio" or "Subscribe free — link in bio"
