# Yarn Shop Collaborations

## Strategy

Partner with independent, online-first yarn shops for mutual promotion: we feature them in our newsletter (yarn spotlights, shop profiles, exclusive discount codes) and they mention Purl of Wisdom in their email marketing or on their website.

This is a zero-cost, high-trust growth channel. Yarn shop customers are exactly our target audience — active knitters who invest in quality materials and want to improve their skills.

---

## Outreach Email Template

**Subject line options:**
- "Collaboration idea for [Shop Name] × Purl of Wisdom"
- "Let's share our knitting communities — partnership idea"
- "Quick question from a fellow knitting business"

---

**Email body:**

Hi [First Name],

I'm Inna, and I run **Purl of Wisdom** — a weekly knitting newsletter where I share technique tips, yarn deep-dives, and project inspiration with a growing community of passionate knitters.

I've been a fan of [Shop Name] for a while — [specific compliment: "your hand-dyed colourways are stunning" / "your beginner kit bundles are so thoughtfully curated" / "I love how you spotlight indie dyers"]. Your shop is exactly the kind of place I'd recommend to my readers.

**Here's what I'm thinking:**

I'd love to feature [Shop Name] in an upcoming issue of Purl of Wisdom — either as a yarn spotlight, a shop profile, or a curated recommendation. In return, I'd be thrilled if you'd mention Purl of Wisdom in one of your emails or on your site — even just a quick "we love this newsletter" blurb.

No cost to either of us — just two knitting businesses sharing audiences that would genuinely appreciate each other.

**A few quick details about Purl of Wisdom:**
- Weekly newsletter for intermediate knitters leveling up their skills
- Consistent 45%+ open rates
- Warm, educational tone — no hard sells or spam
- Topics: technique deep-dives, yarn reviews, pattern tips, knitting culture

If this sounds interesting, I'd love to chat about what would work best for you. No pressure at all — just wanted to plant the seed.

Happy knitting,
Inna
Purl of Wisdom
[newsletter URL]

### Selection Criteria

- **Online-first or strong online presence** — their customers are comfortable engaging digitally
- **Active email list** — they already communicate with customers via email
- **Audience alignment** — customers who knit, value quality yarn, and want to learn
- **Brand quality** — shops we'd genuinely recommend to our readers
- **Approachable size** — large enough to have reach, small enough to say yes to a collaboration

## Collaboration Formats

| Format | What We Give | What We Get |
|---|---|---|
| **Yarn Spotlight** | Feature one of their yarns in a newsletter issue with an honest mini-review | They mention Purl of Wisdom in an email blast or on their blog |
| **Shop Profile** | Dedicated "Shop We Love" section in an issue (200–300 words) | They add our signup link to their website or email footer |
| **Exclusive Discount** | We promote a discount code to our subscribers (drives them sales) | They include our newsletter CTA on the discount landing page |
| **Co-created Content** | Joint blog post or "staff picks" feature | Shared promotion across both audiences |
| **Giveaway** | We run a yarn giveaway in our newsletter (they donate yarn) | Giveaway entry requires newsletter signup — grows both lists |
