# Lead Magnet Setup — The Everyday Market Bag Pattern

## Overview

The Everyday Market Bag is our first lead magnet — a free knitting pattern gated behind email signup. The goal is to convert knitters browsing our content into newsletter subscribers with a high-value, immediately useful freebie.

**Pattern file:** `src/patterns/everyday-market-bag.md`

---

## Beehiiv Integration Steps

### 1. Create the Lead Magnet in Beehiiv

1. **Navigate to:** Beehiiv Dashboard → Grow → Lead Magnets
2. **Click** "Create Lead Magnet"
3. **Configure:**
   - **Name:** The Everyday Market Bag — Free Pattern
   - **Description:** A free knitting pattern for a sturdy, stretchy market bag. Perfect for beginners ready to try mesh knitting.
   - **File:** Upload the pattern as a PDF (export the markdown to a styled PDF first — see section below)
   - **Delivery method:** Automatic email after confirmation
   - **Thank-you message:** "Your pattern is on its way! Check your inbox for The Everyday Market Bag pattern. While you wait, here's what to expect from Purl of Wisdom: one email per week packed with knitting tips, technique deep-dives, and the occasional free pattern. Welcome to the community! — Inna"

### 2. Export Pattern to PDF

Before uploading to Beehiiv, convert the markdown pattern to a polished PDF:

1. Use a markdown-to-PDF tool (Pandoc, Marked 2, or a service like md-to-pdf)
2. Add the Purl of Wisdom header/logo at the top
3. Ensure the schematic renders cleanly
4. Include the newsletter CTA and subscribe link on the final page
5. Target: 4–6 pages, clean and printable

### 3. Create a Dedicated Landing Page

1. **In Beehiiv:** Pages → Create New Page
2. **URL slug:** `/free-market-bag-pattern`
3. **Page content:**
   - Hero: "Knit Your Own Everyday Market Bag — Free Pattern"
   - Sub-headline: "A satisfying, stretchy bag you'll use every week. Get the free pattern delivered to your inbox."
   - Preview image: Photo of the finished bag (or a styled flat-lay of the yarn + needles)
   - Signup form connected to the lead magnet
   - Social proof: "Join [X] knitters who get weekly tips from Purl of Wisdom"
4. **SEO settings:**
   - Title: "Free Knitting Pattern: The Everyday Market Bag | Purl of Wisdom"
   - Description: "Download a free knitting pattern for a stretchy cotton market bag. Perfect for confident beginners. Subscribe to Purl of Wisdom for weekly knitting tips."

### 4. Connect the Signup Flow

1. **New subscriber tag:** Add tag `lead-magnet-market-bag` to anyone who subscribes via this form
2. **Welcome automation:** Ensure the standard welcome sequence still fires after the lead magnet delivery email
3. **Double opt-in:** Keep double opt-in enabled — the lead magnet delivers after confirmation

### 5. Promote the Lead Magnet

| Channel | Action |
|---|---|
| **Newsletter** | Mention in the next 2–3 issues: "Know someone who'd love a free market bag pattern? Share this link!" |
| **Instagram** | Post a reel or carousel showing the finished bag. Link in bio → landing page |
| **Pinterest** | Create 3–4 pins with different images, all linking to the landing page |
| **Ravelry** | Share in relevant forums (Market Bags, Free Patterns, Beginner Knitting) |
| **Reddit** | Post in r/knitting as a free pattern share (follow subreddit self-promo rules) |
| **Blog posts** | Add CTA boxes linking to the lead magnet at the end of both SEO blog posts |
| **Cross-promotions** | Include the lead magnet link in newsletter swap copy |

### 6. Track Performance

**Key metrics to monitor:**

- Landing page conversion rate (target: >30%)
- Lead magnet email open rate (target: >60%)
- Subscriber retention at 30 days (target: >85%)
- Referral source breakdown (which channel drives the most signups?)

**Beehiiv analytics:** Dashboard → Grow → Lead Magnets → The Everyday Market Bag
