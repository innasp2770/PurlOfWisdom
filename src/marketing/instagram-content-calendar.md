## Content Guidelines

- **Visual style:** Warm, natural light. Neutral backgrounds (linen, wood, marble). Hands-in-frame for technique shots. No heavy filters — keep it authentic.
- **Hashtags (use 15–20 per post):** #knitting #knittersofinstagram #knittingcommunity #knitstagram #yarnlove #yarnaddict #handknit #slowfashion #knitlife #makersmovement #fiberarts #woolyarn #knittingpattern #knittingtips #purlofwisdom
- **Stories:** Repost every feed post to stories with a "Subscribe" link sticker. Add polls and questions to boost engagement.
- **Engagement:** Reply to every comment within 2 hours of posting. Like and comment on 10 knitting accounts daily to build community presence.
- **Cross-reference:** Every Thursday Reel should tie to that week's newsletter issue and include "link in bio" CTA.
