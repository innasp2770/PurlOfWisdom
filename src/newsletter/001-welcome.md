## This Week's Pattern Pick

### Cozy Weekender Scarf

A simple but satisfying garter stitch scarf with a twist — literally. A half-twist join creates a beautiful mobius loop that sits perfectly around your neck.

**Skill level:** Beginner
**Yarn:** Any worsted weight, ~200 yards
**Needles:** US 8 (5mm) circular, 32"

This is the kind of project you can knit while watching a show, chatting with friends, or sitting in a cozy corner with tea. No complicated charts, no counting — just the meditative rhythm of knit stitch after knit stitch.

## Technique of the Week

### The Long-Tail Cast On (done right)

Most knitters learn the long-tail cast on first, but did you know there's a way to make it even more elastic? Try this: instead of wrapping the yarn once around your thumb, wrap it twice. This "double long-tail" creates a stretchier edge that's perfect for hat brims and sock cuffs.

## Why Purl of Wisdom?

We started this newsletter because we believe knitting deserves more than Instagram reels and algorithm-driven content. We want a quiet corner of the internet where knitters can learn, discover, and feel inspired — without the noise.

Every issue is written by hand (well, typed — but you get the idea). No AI-generated filler. No sponsored content masquerading as editorial. Just honest, useful knitting content from people who actually knit.
